Setup Guide

Four steps to your first system

Takes less than an afternoon. No technical skills needed.

1
Set up sleep tracking

Wear your tracker to bed for 3–5 nights to establish your baseline sleep score and average wake time.

2
Schedule lights to dim at night

Set your smart bulbs to automatically dim to 10% warm light one hour before your target bedtime.

3
Set your morning light routine

Schedule lights to gradually brighten to 100% cool white starting 20 minutes before your alarm — a natural sunrise effect.

4
Connect your smart plug

Put your bedside lamp or white noise machine on the smart plug. Set it to turn off 30 minutes after your sleep target.
